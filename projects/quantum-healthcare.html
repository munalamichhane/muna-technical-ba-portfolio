Quantum AI in Healthcare

Overview

This research project examines the role of Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI), quantum computing, and quantum sensing technologies in advancing healthcare applications. The work focuses on emerging approaches to medical diagnostics, biomarker discovery, and neurological disease research, with particular emphasis on Alzheimer’s disease.

Problem / Objective

Traditional computational approaches face limitations when modeling complex biological systems and neurological conditions. This research explores how quantum-based techniques may help address these challenges by enabling more efficient data processing and enhanced analytical capabilities.

Approach & Implementation

  • Conducted a structured literature review of peer-reviewed academic research.
  • Analyzed potential use cases of quantum computing and sensing in healthcare diagnostics.
  • Evaluated ethical, technical, and feasibility considerations.
  • Synthesized findings into a formal academic research paper.

What This Project Demonstrates

  • Strong research methodology and academic writing skills.
  • Critical analysis of emerging and experimental technologies.
  • Understanding of healthcare analytics and ethical implications.
  • Ability to translate complex theoretical concepts into structured insights.
