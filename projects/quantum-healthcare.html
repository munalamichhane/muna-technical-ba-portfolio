Quantum AI in Healthcare – Research Project

Overview

This research project examines the role of Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI), quantum computing, and quantum sensing technologies in advancing healthcare applications. It focuses on emerging approaches to medical diagnostics, biomarker discovery, and neurological disease research, with particular emphasis on Alzheimer’s disease.

Problem / Objective

Traditional computational approaches face limitations when modeling complex biological systems and neurological conditions. This research explores how quantum-based techniques may overcome these challenges by enabling more efficient data processing and enhanced analytical capabilities.

Approach & Implementation

  • Conducted a structured literature review of peer-reviewed research.
  • Analyzed practical use cases of quantum computing in healthcare diagnostics.
  • Evaluated ethical considerations and feasibility challenges.
  • Synthesized findings into a formal academic research paper.

What This Project Demonstrates

  • Research rigor and advanced technical writing ability.
  • Critical analysis of emerging technologies.
  • Strong understanding of healthcare analytics and ethics.
  • Ability to translate complex theory into structured, actionable insights.
