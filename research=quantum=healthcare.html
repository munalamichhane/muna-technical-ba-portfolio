Quantum AI in Healthcare Applications

Literature Review on Diagnostics, Biomarker Discovery, and Neurological Research

Overview

This research explores the emerging role of Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI), quantum computing, and quantum sensors in healthcare. The study focuses on advancements in early disease diagnosis, personalized medicine, and neurological research, particularly Alzheimer’s disease.

Research Focus Areas

  • Quantum AI for medical diagnostics and prediction
  • Quantum computing for biomarker discovery
  • Quantum sensors in non-invasive medical imaging
  • Ethical, policy, and sustainability considerations

Key Contributions

  • Reviewed applications of Quantum AI in neurological disease detection
  • Analyzed quantum approaches to genetic and imaging data
  • Evaluated ethical risks related to data privacy and equitable access
  • Identified future opportunities in personalized quantum-driven healthcare

Tools & Concepts

Quantum Computing · Quantum AI · Quantum Neural Networks · Healthcare Analytics · Medical Imaging · Academic Research
